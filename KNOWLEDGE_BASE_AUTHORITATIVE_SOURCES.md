## CATEGORY 3: ACCOUNTING FRAMEWORKS (3)

### 3.1 AAOIFI (Islamic Accounting Standards)

**Primary Source:**
- **AAOIFI Complete Standards Collection**
- **URL:** https://aaoifi.com
- **Access:** Subscription required (~$500-1000/year for institutional access)

**Coverage:**
- 62+ Financial Accounting Standards (FAS)
- 65+ Shariah Standards (SS)
- Governance standards
- Ethics standards
- Audit standards

**Key Standards:**
- FAS 4 - Musharaka Financing
- FAS 28 - Murabaha
- FAS 33 - Investment Sukuk
- SS 9 - Ijara
- SS 40 - Mudaraba profit distribution
- SS 62 - Sukuk

---

### 3.2 IFRS + Islamic (Hybrid Framework)

**Primary Sources:**

**1. "Comparative Accounting of Mudaraba Financing: AAOIFI vs IFRS"**
- **URL:** https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5115905
- **Access:** Free (SSRN working paper)
- **Authors:** Academic research on reconciling IFRS and AAOIFI

**2. "An Investigation on the Applicability of IFRS and AAOIFI Accounting Standards"**
- **URL:** https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5113989
- **Access:** Free (SSRN working paper)

**Content:**
- Mapping IFRS standards to Islamic finance transactions
- Key differences in revenue recognition
- Fair value measurement approaches
- Consolidation of SPVs in sukuk structures

**Supplementary:**
- PWC/Deloitte/EY guides on IFRS for Islamic banks
- Central bank guidance (Malaysia, UAE) on IFRS adoption

---

### 3.3 Local GAAP (Jurisdiction-Specific)

**Coverage:** See jurisdiction-specific regulatory sources above

**Key Jurisdictions:**
- **Malaysia:** Malaysian Financial Reporting Standards (MFRS) + SC/BNM guidance

- **Saudi:** SOCPA standards + CMA requirements
- **Qatar:** QFC requirements (IFRS-based)
