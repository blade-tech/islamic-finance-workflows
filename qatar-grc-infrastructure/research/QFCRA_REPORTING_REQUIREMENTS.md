# QFCRA Regulatory Reporting Requirements

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive catalog of all regulatory reporting requirements for Islamic Financial Institutions operating under QFCRA jurisdiction. It details periodic reports, event-driven notifications, SSB reporting obligations, and public disclosure requirements with specific deadlines, content requirements, and submission formats.

**Document Version**: 1.0
**Last Updated**: November 2024
**Primary Sources**:
- Islamic Finance Rules 2005 (ISFI) Version 9
- Islamic Banking Business Prudential Rules 2015 (IBANK) Version 11
- Governance and Controlled Functions Rules 2020 (CTRL) Version 7
- Banking Business Prudential Rules 2014 (BANK)

---

## Overview of Reporting Framework

### Reporting Categories

1. **Periodic Reports** - Regular submissions on fixed schedule
2. **Event-Driven Reports** - Triggered by specific incidents
3. **SSB Reports** - Shariah governance specific
4. **Public Disclosures** - Market transparency requirements
5. **Ad-Hoc Reports** - Upon QFCRA request

### Reporting Channels

- **QFCRA Portal**: Primary submission platform
- **Email**: For urgent notifications
- **Physical Submission**: For signed originals where required
- **Public Platform**: Website/annual reports for disclosures

## Periodic Reports

### PR-01: Quarterly Prudential Returns

- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**:
  - Capital adequacy calculations
  - Risk-weighted assets
  - Large exposures
  - Liquidity ratios
  - Profit rate risk in banking book
  - Investment risk reserves
- **Deadline**: Within 20 business days after quarter end
- **Format**: QFCRA prescribed templates (Excel-based)
- **Source**: IBANK Chapter 2
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### PR-02: Monthly Statistical Returns

- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Content**:
  - Balance sheet summary
  - Profit and loss summary
  - Deposit analysis
  - Financing portfolio breakdown
  - Investment account details
- **Deadline**: Within 15 business days after month end
- **Format**: QFCRA statistical templates
- **Source**: IBANK Rule 2.1.1
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### PR-03: Annual Audited Financial Statements

- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Content**:
  - Complete audited financial statements
  - External auditor's report
  - Management letter
  - Shariah compliance disclosures (per IBANK 11.1.9)
- **Deadline**: Within 4 months after financial year end
- **Format**: PDF with signed originals to follow
- **Source**: GENE and IBANK requirements
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal + Physical

### PR-04: Annual Shariah Supervisory Board Report

- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Content** (per AAOIFI GSIFI No. 1):
  - SSB opinion on Shariah compliance
  - Scope of reviews conducted
  - Procedures performed
  - Key findings and observations
  - Recommendations
  - Statement of management responsibility
- **Deadline**: Within 3 months after financial year end
- **Format**: Signed original document
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.2.2
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal + Physical original

### PR-05: Semi-Annual Risk Report

- **Frequency**: Semi-annual
- **Content**:
  - Risk profile assessment
  - Key risk indicators
  - Risk appetite statement compliance
  - Stress testing results
  - Operational risk events summary
- **Deadline**: Within 30 business days after period end
- **Format**: QFCRA risk reporting template
- **Source**: CTRL Chapter 8
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### PR-06: Quarterly Investment Account Report

- **Frequency**: Quarterly (for Islamic banks with PSIAs)
- **Content**:
  - PSIA balances by type
  - Profit distribution calculations
  - Investment performance
  - Asset allocation
  - Displaced commercial risk assessment
- **Deadline**: Within 20 business days after quarter end
- **Format**: QFCRA PSIA template
- **Source**: IBANK specific requirements
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### PR-07: Annual Internal Shariah Review Report

- **Frequency**: Annual (or as determined by SSB)
- **Content**:
  - Scope of internal review
  - Methodology applied
  - Compliance assessment results
  - Exceptions identified
  - Corrective actions taken
- **Deadline**: Within 30 days of completion
- **Format**: Detailed report format
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.3.1
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### PR-08: Quarterly Operational Loss Data Report

- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**:
  - Operational losses by event type
  - Recovery amounts
  - Near miss events
  - Loss trends analysis
- **Deadline**: Within 20 business days after quarter end
- **Format**: QFCRA operational risk template
- **Source**: IBANK Rule 7.3.1
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

## Event-Driven Reports

### ED-01: Operational Risk Event Notification

- **Trigger**: Operational risk event or near miss with material impact
- **Timeline**: Within 1 business day of occurrence
- **Content**:
  - Nature and details of event
  - Impact on operations
  - Initial response actions
  - Estimated losses
  - Remediation plan
- **Source**: CTRL Rule 8.1.2
- **Submission Method**: Email + Portal follow-up

### ED-02: Capital Adequacy Breach Notification

- **Trigger**: Breach or anticipated breach of minimum capital requirements
- **Timeline**: As soon as practicable (same day if critical)
- **Content**:
  - Current capital ratios
  - Nature of breach
  - Causes identified
  - Remediation plan
  - Timeline for restoration
- **Source**: IBANK Rule 2.1.8
- **Submission Method**: Immediate email + formal letter

### ED-03: Significant SNCR Event Report

- **Trigger**: Material Shariah non-compliance event
- **Timeline**: Within 3 business days of discovery
- **Content**:
  - Description of non-compliance
  - Root cause analysis
  - Financial impact
  - SSB assessment
  - Purification requirements
  - Remediation actions
- **Source**: Implied from operational risk requirements
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### ED-04: Large Exposure Breach Notification

- **Trigger**: Breach of large exposure limits
- **Timeline**: Immediately upon occurrence
- **Content**:
  - Counterparty details
  - Exposure amount
  - Limit breached
  - Mitigation actions
  - Timeline for compliance
- **Source**: IBANK prudential requirements
- **Submission Method**: Email + Portal

### ED-05: Liquidity Stress Event Report

- **Trigger**: Liquidity ratio breach or stress event
- **Timeline**: Same business day
- **Content**:
  - Current liquidity position
  - Stress factors
  - Contingency funding plan activation
  - Expected resolution timeline
- **Source**: IBANK liquidity requirements
- **Submission Method**: Immediate notification

### ED-06: SSB Composition Change Notification

- **Trigger**: Appointment, resignation, or dismissal of SSB member
- **Timeline**: Within 5 business days
- **Content**:
  - Member details
  - Reason for change
  - Replacement plans
  - Continuity arrangements
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.1.6
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### ED-07: Material Outsourcing Notification

- **Trigger**: New material outsourcing arrangement
- **Timeline**: 30 days before implementation
- **Content**:
  - Service provider details
  - Services outsourced
  - Risk assessment
  - Contingency plans
  - Shariah compliance considerations
- **Source**: CTRL outsourcing requirements
- **Submission Method**: QFCRA Portal

### ED-08: Significant Litigation Report

- **Trigger**: Material litigation or regulatory action
- **Timeline**: Within 5 business days of initiation
- **Content**:
  - Nature of litigation
  - Potential financial impact
  - Legal opinion
  - Provision requirements
- **Source**: General notification requirements
- **Submission Method**: Formal letter

## SSB Reports

### SSB-01: Annual SSB Compliance Opinion

- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Content**:
  - Overall Shariah compliance opinion
  - Basis for opinion
  - Qualifications or exceptions
  - Key recommendations
- **Deadline**: With annual financial statements
- **Format**: Signed formal opinion
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.2.2
- **Recipients**: QFCRA, Board, Shareholders

### SSB-02: Quarterly SSB Activity Report

- **Frequency**: Quarterly (best practice)
- **Content**:
  - Meetings held
  - Key decisions made
  - Products reviewed
  - Fatwa issued
  - Training conducted
- **Deadline**: Within 15 days after quarter end
- **Format**: Summary report
- **Source**: Best practice
- **Recipients**: Board and Management

### SSB-03: Product Approval Notifications

- **Frequency**: Event-driven
- **Content**:
  - Product description
  - Shariah structure
  - SSB approval/conditions
  - Implementation requirements
- **Deadline**: Before product launch
- **Format**: Formal fatwa document
- **Source**: ISFI Chapter 6
- **Recipients**: QFCRA (for information)

### SSB-04: SNCR Resolution Reports

- **Frequency**: Event-driven
- **Content**:
  - Incident description
  - Shariah analysis
  - Purification determination
  - Preventive measures
- **Deadline**: Within 30 days of SSB decision
- **Format**: Detailed resolution
- **Source**: SSB governance requirements
- **Recipients**: QFCRA, Management

## Public Disclosures

### PD-01: Annual Report Disclosures

- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Content**:
  - SSB members and qualifications
  - SSB report to shareholders
  - Shariah compliance statement
  - Zakat information
  - Charity and purification amounts
- **Deadline**: With annual report publication
- **Format**: Published annual report
- **Source**: ISFI and IBANK disclosure requirements
- **Medium**: Company website, printed reports

### PD-02: Financial Statement Disclosures

- **Frequency**: Annual and interim
- **Content** (per IBANK 11.1.9):
  - Role and authority of SSB
  - Zakat calculation method
  - Zakat amounts paid
  - Charity fund utilization
  - Earnings prohibited by Shariah
- **Deadline**: With financial statement publication
- **Format**: Notes to financial statements
- **Source**: IBANK Rule 11.1.9
- **Medium**: Published financials

### PD-03: Product Disclosure Statements

- **Frequency**: Ongoing
- **Content**:
  - SSB approval status
  - Shariah structure
  - Risk disclosure
  - Profit distribution mechanism
- **Deadline**: Before product marketing
- **Format**: Product brochures and websites
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.2.3
- **Medium**: Marketing materials

### PD-04: Pillar 3 Disclosures (Islamic Banks)

- **Frequency**: Annual (minimum)
- **Content**:
  - Capital structure
  - Risk exposures
  - Risk management processes
  - Capital adequacy
  - Shariah governance
- **Deadline**: With annual financial statements
- **Format**: Structured disclosure document
- **Source**: Basel III/IFSB requirements
- **Medium**: Company website

## Ad-Hoc Reporting Requirements

### AH-01: QFCRA Information Requests

- **Trigger**: QFCRA specific request
- **Timeline**: As specified (typically 5-10 business days)
- **Content**: As requested
- **Format**: As specified
- **Source**: ISFI Rule 6.1.6 and general supervisory powers

### AH-02: Thematic Reviews

- **Trigger**: QFCRA thematic review initiative
- **Timeline**: As per review timeline
- **Content**: Specific to review theme
- **Format**: QFCRA templates
- **Source**: Supervisory review process

### AH-03: Regulatory Consultations

- **Trigger**: QFCRA consultation papers
- **Timeline**: Typically 30-60 days
- **Content**: Response to proposals
- **Format**: Written submission
- **Source**: Regulatory development process

## Reporting Calendar and Timeline

### Daily Monitoring Requirements
- Capital adequacy levels
- Liquidity positions
- Large exposures
- Operational incidents

### Monthly Submissions
- Statistical returns (15 business days)
- Management accounts (internal)

### Quarterly Submissions
- Prudential returns (20 business days)
- Investment account reports (20 business days)
- Operational loss data (20 business days)
- SSB activity reports (15 days - best practice)

### Semi-Annual Submissions
- Risk reports (30 business days)
- Interim financial statements (if required)

### Annual Submissions
- **Month 1-3 after year-end**:
  - SSB annual report (3 months)
  - Internal Shariah review report

- **Month 4 after year-end**:
  - Audited financial statements
  - Annual report with disclosures
  - Pillar 3 disclosures

### Event-Driven (Immediate)
- Operational risk events (1 business day)
- Capital/liquidity breaches (same day)
- Large exposure breaches (immediate)

## Submission Formats and Templates

### Standard Templates Available

1. **Prudential Returns**
   - Form CAR - Capital Adequacy
   - Form LE - Large Exposures
   - Form LCR - Liquidity Coverage Ratio
   - Form NSFR - Net Stable Funding Ratio

2. **Statistical Returns**
   - Form BS - Balance Sheet
   - Form PL - Profit & Loss
   - Form DEP - Deposits
   - Form FIN - Financing

3. **Risk Reports**
   - Operational Risk Event Form
   - Risk Register Template
   - KRI Dashboard Template

### File Formats Accepted

- **Excel**: For quantitative returns
- **PDF**: For signed documents and reports
- **Word**: For narrative reports (converted to PDF)
- **XML/XBRL**: Future requirement for automated processing

### Naming Conventions

Format: `[Institution]_[Report]_[Period]_[Version]`

Example: `QIB_CAR_2024Q1_V1.xlsx`

## Quality Assurance and Validation

### Pre-Submission Checklist

#### Data Quality
- [ ] All fields completed
- [ ] Calculations verified
- [ ] Consistency checks performed
- [ ] Prior period comparisons done
- [ ] Material changes explained

#### Authorization
- [ ] Internal review completed
- [ ] Appropriate sign-offs obtained
- [ ] SSB approval where required
- [ ] Board approval for annual reports

#### Technical Requirements
- [ ] Correct template version used
- [ ] File naming convention followed
- [ ] File size within limits
- [ ] No corrupted formulas/links

### Common Validation Errors

1. **Arithmetic Errors**
   - Totals not matching
   - Percentage calculations incorrect
   - Currency conversion errors

2. **Logic Errors**
   - Negative values where not allowed
   - Limits exceeded without explanation
   - Inconsistent data between forms

3. **Completeness Issues**
   - Mandatory fields blank
   - Supporting schedules missing
   - Narrative explanations absent

## Penalties and Enforcement

### Late Submission Penalties

1. **First Offense**: Warning letter
2. **Repeated Delays**: Financial penalties
3. **Chronic Non-Compliance**:
   - Public disclosure
   - Business restrictions
   - Board/management accountability

### Incorrect Reporting Consequences

1. **Unintentional Errors**:
   - Resubmission required
   - Root cause analysis

2. **Negligent Reporting**:
   - Financial penalties
   - Enhanced supervision
   - Additional reporting requirements

3. **Intentional Misreporting**:
   - Significant financial penalties
   - Criminal referral
   - License suspension/revocation

- Single Exposure / Capital ≤ 25%

- Aggregate Large Exposures / Capital ≤ 800%
