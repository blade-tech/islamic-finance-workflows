# Qatar Central Bank (QCB) Evidence and Documentation Standards for Islamic Banks

## Executive Summary

QCB mandates comprehensive documentation and evidence requirements for Islamic banks, with **10-year retention periods** (exceeding QFCRA's 6 years) and specific format requirements for Shariah compliance evidence. Banks must maintain both transactional and governance documentation in Arabic and English.

## Legal Framework

### Primary Requirements
- **QCB Law 13/2012**: General documentation obligations
- **Instructions to Banks**: Specific retention and format requirements
- **AAOIFI Standards**: Documentation standards for Islamic products
- **QCB Circular 68/2015**: Governance documentation requirements

## Document Categories and Retention Requirements

### A. SHARIAH GOVERNANCE DOCUMENTATION

#### 1. SSB Decisions and Fatwas
- **Retention Period**: Permanent
- **Format Requirements**:
  - Original signed Arabic version
  - English translation
  - Digital searchable format
- **Content Standards**:
  - Question presented
  - Shariah analysis and evidence
  - Decision and conditions
  - Dissenting opinions (if any)
- **Access Requirements**:
  - Searchable fatwa database
  - Public access to general fatwas
  - Confidential treatment for specific transactions

#### 2. SSB Meeting Documentation
- **Retention Period**: 10 years minimum
- **Required Documents**:
  - Meeting notices and agendas
  - Attendance records
  - Presentation materials
  - Detailed minutes
  - Resolutions and decisions
  - Action items and follow-ups
- **Format**: Original signed copies + digital

#### 3. SSB Annual Reports
- **Retention Period**: Permanent
- **Requirements**:
  - Published version
  - Working papers
  - Management responses
  - QCB correspondence
- **Public Disclosure**: Required in annual report

---

### B. PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

#### 4. Product Approval Documentation
- **Retention Period**: Product life + 10 years
- **Required Evidence**:
  - Product proposal and structure
  - Shariah analysis and basis
  - SSB approval/fatwa
  - Legal opinions
  - Risk assessments
  - Operational procedures
  - Marketing materials
- **Format**: Complete file with version control

#### 5. Contract Documentation (by Product Type)

##### Murabaha Contracts
- **Retention**: Transaction term + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Master Murabaha agreement
  - Purchase order (Wa'ad)
  - Agency agreement (if applicable)
  - Purchase evidence/invoice
  - Offer and acceptance
  - Delivery documentation
  - Payment schedules
- **Sequencing Evidence**: Timestamp proof of proper sequence

##### Ijarah Contracts
- **Retention**: Lease term + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Lease agreement
  - Asset purchase documents
  - Title/ownership proof
  - Insurance policies
  - Maintenance records
  - Promise to purchase (if Ijarah Muntahia)
  - Asset transfer documents
- **Special Requirements**: Annual asset verification

##### Musharaka Contracts
- **Retention**: Partnership term + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Partnership agreement
  - Capital contribution evidence
  - Profit/loss sharing mechanism
  - Management agreements
  - Diminishing schedule (if applicable)
  - Exit documentation
- **Audit Trail**: All partnership decisions documented

##### Wakala Contracts
- **Retention**: Agency term + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Agency agreement
  - Investment mandates
  - Fee agreements
  - Performance reports
  - Asset documentation
- **Monitoring**: Periodic compliance certificates

##### Sukuk Documentation
- **Retention**: Maturity + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Prospectus/offering documents
  - Trust deed
  - Asset documentation
  - SSB pronouncement
  - Rating reports
  - Periodic payment evidence
- **Public Access**: Prospectus publicly available

---

### C. TRANSACTION DOCUMENTATION

#### 6. Customer Documentation
- **Retention Period**: Relationship + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Account opening forms
  - KYC documentation
  - Risk profiling
  - Product disclosures signed
  - Terms acceptance
  - Complaint records
- **Format**: Original signatures + digital copies

#### 7. Transaction Records
- **Retention Period**: 10 years from transaction
- **Requirements**:
  - Complete audit trail
  - Approval hierarchy
  - Shariah compliance check
  - Execution evidence
  - Settlement confirmation
- **System Requirements**: Immutable audit logs

---

### D. COMPLIANCE DOCUMENTATION

#### 8. SNCR Documentation
- **Retention Period**: Permanent for material events
- **Required Evidence**:
  - Incident reports
  - Root cause analysis
  - SSB review and decision
  - Purification calculations
  - Charity distribution receipts
  - Remediation plans
  - Preventive measures
- **Reporting**: Quarterly summary to QCB

#### 9. Shariah Audit Documentation
- **Retention Period**: 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Audit plans and scope
  - Working papers
  - Testing evidence
  - Findings and exceptions
  - Management responses
  - Follow-up actions
  - Clearance certificates
- **Standards**: Follow AAOIFI GSIFI standards

#### 10. Profit Distribution Documentation
- **Retention Period**: 10 years
- **Required Evidence**:
  - Profit calculation sheets
  - Weightage calculations
  - Mudarib share computation
  - PER/IRR movements
  - SSB approval
  - Distribution notices
  - Customer statements
- **Audit Requirements**: Monthly reconciliation

---

### E. FINANCIAL DOCUMENTATION

#### 11. Financial Statements and Reports
- **Retention Period**: Permanent for annual, 10 years for periodic
- **Required Documents**:
  - Draft and final statements
  - Working papers
  - Audit adjustments
  - Management representations
  - QCB correspondence
  - Board approvals
- **Format**: AAOIFI-compliant presentation

#### 12. Investment Documentation
- **Retention Period**: Holding period + 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Investment approval
  - Due diligence reports
  - Valuation documentation
  - Shariah screening evidence
  - Fair value assessments
  - Impairment analysis
  - Disposal documentation
- **Classification Evidence**: FAS 25 compliance

---

### F. REGULATORY DOCUMENTATION

#### 13. Regulatory Submissions
- **Retention Period**: 10 years minimum
- **Required Evidence**:
  - All QCB submissions
  - Supporting calculations
  - Correspondence
  - Approvals/objections
  - Remediation evidence
- **Format**: As submitted + acknowledgments

#### 14. External Audit Documentation
- **Retention Period**: 10 years
- **Required Documents**:
  - Engagement letters
  - Audit reports
  - Management letters
  - Representation letters
  - QCB correspondence
- **Access**: QCB inspection rights

## Documentation Standards and Formats

### Language Requirements
- **Primary**: Arabic for official documents
- **Secondary**: English translations required
- **Legal Precedence**: Arabic version prevails

### Format Standards

#### Physical Documentation
- **Paper**: Archival quality
- **Signatures**: Original wet signatures
- **Certification**: Notarization where required
- **Storage**: Fireproof, secure facilities

#### Digital Documentation
- **Format**: PDF/A for long-term preservation
- **Metadata**: Complete indexing
- **Security**: Encryption and access controls
- **Backup**: Multiple locations, regular testing
- **Integrity**: Hash verification, blockchain optional

### Accessibility Requirements
- **Internal Access**: Role-based permissions
- **Regulatory Access**: Within 24-48 hours
- **Customer Access**: Upon request (own documents)
- **Audit Access**: Read-only access protocols

## Quality Standards

### Documentation Completeness
Each document set must include:
1. Primary documents
2. Supporting evidence
3. Approvals and authorizations
4. Amendments and variations
5. Termination/completion records

### Authentication Requirements
- **Originals**: Maintained for key documents
- **Copies**: Certified as true copies
- **Electronic**: Digital signatures where permitted
- **Foreign Documents**: Apostille/consular attestation

### Version Control
- All document versions retained
- Change tracking mandatory
- Amendment history documented
- Current version clearly marked

## Inspection and Audit Requirements

### QCB Inspection Rights
- **Notice**: May inspect without notice
- **Scope**: All documentation
- **Format**: Original or certified copies
- **Timeline**: Immediate access required
- **Location**: On-site or QCB premises

### External Audit Requirements
- Annual verification of documentation
- Sampling of transaction documents
- Shariah audit of Islamic documentation
- Management letter on gaps

### Internal Audit
- Quarterly documentation review
- Completeness verification
- Retention compliance check
- Quality assessment

## Comparison with QFCRA Standards

| Aspect | QCB | QFCRA |
|--------|-----|-------|
| **Retention Period** | 10 years | 6 years |
| **Language** | Arabic + English | English primary |
| **SSB Documentation** | Permanent | 10 years |
| **Format Requirements** | Highly prescriptive | Principles-based |
| **Digital Standards** | Specific requirements | General guidance |
| **Inspection Rights** | Immediate access | Reasonable notice |
| **Public Disclosure** | Extensive | Limited |

## Penalties for Non-Compliance

### Documentation Failures
- **Missing Documents**: QAR 10,000 per document
- **Incomplete Records**: QAR 50,000 per incident
- **Systemic Failures**: QAR 500,000 + remediation

### Retention Violations
- **Premature Disposal**: QAR 100,000 per batch
- **Lost Documents**: QAR 25,000 per document
- **System Failures**: QAR 250,000 + restoration

### Access Failures
- **Delayed Access**: QAR 10,000 per day
- **Inability to Produce**: QAR 100,000
- **Inspection Obstruction**: Criminal referral

### Governance Framework
1. **Documentation Policy**: Comprehensive policy covering all requirements
2. **Roles and Responsibilities**: Clear ownership
3. **Monitoring and Reporting**: Regular compliance assessment
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Regular updates based on findings
