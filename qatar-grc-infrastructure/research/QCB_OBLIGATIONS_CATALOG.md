# Qatar Central Bank (QCB) Islamic Finance Obligations Catalog

## Executive Summary

This catalog documents **45+ regulatory obligations** for Islamic financial institutions operating under QCB supervision in mainland Qatar. These obligations are more comprehensive than QFCRA's 28 obligations, reflecting QCB's role as the primary banking regulator with detailed prescriptive requirements.

## Catalog Structure

Each obligation includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier (OBL-QCB-###)
- **Category**: Regulatory domain
- **Source**: Specific QCB regulation/article
- **Requirement**: Detailed obligation description
- **Frequency**: One-time/Ongoing/Periodic
- **Evidence Required**: Documentation needed
- **Related Controls**: From 26-control library
- **QFCRA Comparison**: How it differs from QFC requirements

## 1. SHARIAH GOVERNANCE OBLIGATIONS

### OBL-QCB-001: Establish Shariah Supervisory Board
- **Source**: QCB Law 13/2012, Article 109
- **Requirement**: Establish SSB with minimum 3 qualified Shariah scholars
- **Applicability**: All Islamic banks and Islamic windows
- **Frequency**: One-time establishment, ongoing maintenance
- **Evidence Required**: SSB appointment letters, CVs, Board resolutions
- **Penalty**: License revocation risk
- **Related Controls**: CTRL-01 (SSB Establishment)
- **QFCRA Comparison**: Similar requirement but QCB more prescriptive

### OBL-QCB-002: SSB Independence
- **Source**: QCB Circular 68/2015, Corporate Governance
- **Requirement**: Ensure SSB members are independent of management
- **Applicability**: All Islamic banks
- **Frequency**: Ongoing
- **Evidence Required**: Independence declarations, conflict of interest policies
- **Penalty**: Governance violations
- **Related Controls**: CTRL-02 (SSB Independence)
- **QFCRA Comparison**: QCB has stricter independence criteria

### OBL-QCB-003: SSB Annual Report
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Chapter 8
- **Requirement**: SSB must issue annual Shariah compliance report
- **Applicability**: All Islamic banks
- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Evidence Required**: Published SSB report in annual statements
- **Penalty**: Financial statements rejection
- **Related Controls**: CTRL-04 (SSB Reporting)
- **QFCRA Comparison**: QCB requires more detailed disclosures

### OBL-QCB-004: Internal Shariah Compliance Function
- **Source**: QCB Circular 68/2015
- **Requirement**: Establish internal Shariah compliance department
- **Applicability**: All Islamic banks
- **Frequency**: Ongoing

- **Penalty**: Operational restrictions
- **Related Controls**: CTRL-05 (Internal Shariah Compliance)
- **QFCRA Comparison**: QCB requires dedicated department vs. function

### OBL-QCB-005: Shariah Audit
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks
- **Requirement**: Conduct annual Shariah audit of all Islamic operations
- **Applicability**: All Islamic banks
- **Frequency**: Annual minimum
- **Evidence Required**: Shariah audit reports, findings, remediation
