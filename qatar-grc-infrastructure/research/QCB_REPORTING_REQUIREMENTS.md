# Qatar Central Bank (QCB) Reporting Requirements for Islamic Banks

## Executive Summary

Islamic banks operating under QCB supervision must submit **35+ regular reports** covering financial, prudential, Shariah compliance, and operational aspects. This exceeds QFCRA's 20 reporting requirements, reflecting QCB's more granular supervisory approach.

## Reporting Framework Overview

### Submission Channels
- **QCB Portal**: Primary electronic submission platform
- **Secure Email**: For confidential reports
- **Physical Submission**: For signed annual documents
- **XBRL Format**: For certain financial reports

### General Requirements
- All reports must be in Arabic and English
- CEO/CFO certification required for financial reports
- External auditor validation for annual reports
- Penalties for late submission (QAR 10,000-100,000 per day)

## A. FINANCIAL REPORTING

### 1. Monthly Balance Sheet
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Deadline**: 8th of following month (national banks, phase 1)
- **Content**:
  - Assets, liabilities, off-balance sheet items
  - Income statement
  - 45+ detailed breakdown tables
- **Format**: QCB prescribed template with annexes 62-107
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Ch.8, Section 1/1

### 2. Consolidated Balance Sheet - Branches
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Deadline**: 12th of following month (phase 2)
- **Content**:
  - Each foreign branch separately
  - Islamic vs conventional segregation
  - Country-wise consolidation
- **Format**: Same as monthly balance sheet
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Section 1/2

### 3. Consolidated Balance Sheet - Subsidiaries
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Deadline**: 25th of following month (phase 3)
- **Content**:
  - Each subsidiary inside/outside Qatar
  - Bank group consolidation
  - Full group consolidation
- **Format**: QCB templates
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Section 1/2

### 4. Quarterly Financial Statements
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Deadline**: 45 days after quarter end
- **Content**:
  - Comprehensive financial statements
  - AAOIFI compliant format
  - Unaudited but reviewed
- **Format**: AAOIFI templates
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, IAS 34

### 5. Annual Financial Statements
- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Deadline**: Within 3 months, QCB approval required
- **Content**:
  - Full AAOIFI-compliant statements
  - Auditor's report
  - SSB report
  - All required disclosures
- **Format**: AAOIFI illustrative statements (Annex 131)
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Section 2/5

## B. PRUDENTIAL REPORTING

### 6. Capital Adequacy Report
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Deadline**: 15th of following month
- **Content**:
  - CAR calculation per Basel III
  - Risk-weighted assets breakdown
  - Capital components
  - Buffers calculation
- **Format**: QCB Basel III templates
- **Source**: Basel III Implementation Instructions

### 7. Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)
- **Frequency**: Monthly (daily monitoring required)
- **Deadline**: 10th of following month
- **Content**:
  - HQLA composition
  - Net cash outflows
  - LCR calculation
  - Stress scenarios
- **Format**: QCB LCR template
- **Source**: QCB Liquidity Framework

### 8. Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR)
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Deadline**: 15 days after quarter end
- **Content**:
  - Available stable funding
  - Required stable funding
  - NSFR calculation by maturity
- **Format**: QCB NSFR template
- **Source**: Basel III Framework

### 9. Large Exposure Report
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Deadline**: 15th of following month
- **Content**:
  - All exposures >10% of capital
  - Connected party analysis
  - Concentration by sector
  - Breach notifications
- **Format**: QCB prescribed format
- **Source**: QCB Prudential Regulations

### 10. Country Risk Report
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Deadline**: 20 days after quarter end
- **Content**:
  - Cross-border exposures
  - Country risk ratings
  - Provisions by country
- **Format**: QCB template
- **Source**: QCB Risk Management Guidelines

## C. SHARIAH COMPLIANCE REPORTING

### 11. SSB Annual Report
- **Frequency**: Annual
- **Deadline**: With annual financial statements
- **Content**:
  - Shariah compliance opinion
  - Review of operations
  - Product approvals
  - SNCR summary
  - Recommendations
- **Format**: Free format per AAOIFI GS-1
- **Source**: QCB Shariah Governance Requirements

### 12. SNCR Quarterly Report
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Deadline**: 15 days after quarter end
- **Content**:
  - All SNCR events in period
  - Root cause analysis
  - Purification amounts
  - Remediation status
  - Prevention measures
- **Format**: QCB SNCR template
- **Source**: QCB Shariah Compliance Guidelines

### 13. Significant SNCR Incident Report
- **Frequency**: Event-driven
- **Deadline**: Within 5 business days
- **Content**:
  - Incident description
  - Financial impact
  - Customer impact
  - Immediate actions
  - Investigation plan
- **Format**: Detailed incident report
- **Source**: QCB Supervisory Requirements

### 14. Profit Distribution Report
- **Frequency**: Monthly/Quarterly (per distribution)
- **Deadline**: Before distribution
- **Content**:
  - Profit calculation methodology
  - Distribution rates
  - Mudarib share
  - PER/IRR movements
  - SSB approval
- **Format**: QCB Form for profit distribution
- **Source**: Instructions to Banks, Second

### 15. Purification Report
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Deadline**: 15 days after quarter end
- **Content**:
  - Non-compliant income identified
  - Purification calculations
  - Charity distributions
  - SSB approval
- **Format**: Bank template, QCB review
- **Source**: Shariah Requirements

- **Triggers**:
  - Capital breach
  - Liquidity stress
  - Major SNCR
  - System failures
  - Fraud events
  - Management changes

  - Limit breaches
  - Ratio violations

- **Deadline**: Within 2 business days
- **Format**: Breach notification template

## Reporting Calendar Summary

### Daily Requirements
- Internal monitoring (not reported unless breach)

### Weekly Requirements
- Internal liquidity monitoring

### Monthly Requirements (15 reports)
- Due by 8th: Balance sheet
- Due by 10th: LCR, Monetary stats
- Due by 12th: Branch consolidation
- Due by 15th: CAR, Market risk, Investment accounts
- Due by 20th: Credit risk
- Due by 25th: Full consolidation

### Quarterly Requirements (12 reports)
- Financial statements
- NSFR
- Risk reports
- SNCR report
- Product reports
- Statistical returns

### Annual Requirements (8 reports)
- Audited financials
- SSB report
- Business plan
- ICAAP
- Recovery plan
- Various assessments

## Comparison with QFCRA Requirements

| Aspect | QCB | QFCRA |
|--------|-----|-------|
| **Total Reports** | 35+ | 20 |
| **Monthly Reports** | 15 | 5 |
| **Quarterly Reports** | 12 | 8 |
| **Annual Reports** | 8 | 7 |
| **Shariah-Specific** | 5 | 3 |
| **Submission Platform** | QCB Portal | QFCRA Systems |
| **Language** | Arabic + English | English primary |
| **Late Penalties** | Higher | Lower |

## Key Challenges and Recommendations

### Challenges
1. **High frequency**: Monthly reporting burden
2. **Multiple deadlines**: Complex calendar management
3. **Manual processes**: Some reports still manual
4. **Reconciliation**: Between different reports
5. **Bilingual requirements**: Translation needs

### Recommendations
1. **Automation**: Invest in regulatory reporting systems
2. **Calendar management**: Centralized reporting calendar
3. **Quality control**: Multi-level review process
4. **Documentation**: Clear procedures and responsibilities
5. **Training**: Regular staff training on requirements

## Penalties for Non-Compliance

### Late Submission
- First day: QAR 10,000
- Per additional day: QAR 5,000
- Maximum: QAR 100,000 per report

### Incorrect Submission
- Minor errors: Warning
- Material errors: QAR 50,000
- Repeated errors: QAR 100,000

### Non-Submission
- First instance: QAR 100,000
- Repeated: QAR 500,000
- Chronic: License action
