## Detailed Control Mapping

### Bucket 1: Shariah Governance & Compliance

#### Control SG-01: SSB Mandate & Fatwa Issuance

**Activation**:
- QCB: ✓ Mandatory
- QFCRA: ✓ Mandatory

**Mapped Qatar Obligations**:
1. UNIFIED-OBL-001: Establish SSB (minimum 3 scholars)
2. UNIFIED-OBL-003: SSB Annual Report
3. UNIFIED-OBL-013: New Product SSB Approval

**QCB-Specific Requirements**:
- Minimum 3 members required
- Maximum 3 SSB positions per scholar
- 2-year cooling-off period
- Annual SSB report in detailed template
- Product fatwa before launch

**QFCRA-Specific Requirements**:
- Minimum 3 members required
- Competence assessment documented
- Annual report per AAOIFI GS-1
- Submit to QFCRA within 3 months

**Control Test Procedures (QCB)**:
1. Verify SSB charter exists and is current
2. Confirm minimum 3 members appointed
3. Check each member holds maximum 3 SSB positions
4. Review member qualifications against criteria
5. Verify independence declarations signed
6. Confirm all products have valid fatwas
7. Check fatwa register completeness

**Control Test Procedures (QFCRA)**:
1. Verify SSB appointment documentation
2. Review competence assessments
3. Check independence requirements met
4. Verify annual report submitted to QFCRA
5. Confirm product approvals documented

**Evidence Required**:
- QCB: SSB charter, appointment letters, CVs, independence declarations, fatwa register, annual report
- QFCRA: Appointment documents, competence assessments, QFCRA submission confirmations

**Testing Frequency**:
- QCB: Quarterly for appointments, annual for report
- QFCRA: Annual comprehensive review

**KRI/KCI**:
- SSB vacancy duration (days)
- SSB meeting attendance rate (%)
- Average fatwa turnaround time (days)
- Products without valid fatwa (count)
- SSB report submission timeliness

## Control Activation Decision Tree

### For QCB Entities

**Mandatory Activation** (Must activate all):

1. **Bucket 1 (Shariah Governance)**: ALL 5 controls
   - SG-01 through SG-05

2. **Bucket 2 (Regulatory & Legal)**: ALL 5 controls
   - RL-01 through RL-05
   - Plus NEW CONTROLS 1, 3, 6

3. **Bucket 3 (Risk Management)**: ALL 5 controls
   - RM-01 through RM-05
   - Plus NEW CONTROL 5 (DCR)

4. **Bucket 4 (Financial & Product Reporting)**: ALL 6 controls
   - FR-01 through FR-06
   - Plus NEW CONTROLS 2, 4

5. **Bucket 5 (Audit & Assurance)**: ALL 5 controls
   - AA-01 through AA-05
   - Plus NEW CONTROL 7

**Total for QCB**: 26 existing + 8 new = 34 controls

### For QFCRA Entities

**Mandatory Activation**:

1. **Bucket 1**: All except enhanced QCB requirements
   - SG-01 through SG-05 (with QFCRA parameters)

2. **Bucket 2**: Core controls
   - RL-01 through RL-05 (English only)

3. **Bucket 3**: Risk controls with flexibility
   - RM-01 through RM-05 (principles-based)

4. **Bucket 4**: Adapted reporting
   - FR-01, FR-02 (quarterly focus)
   - FR-03 (selective AAOIFI)
   - FR-04 (flexible methodology)
   - FR-05 (if applicable)
   - FR-06 (for windows)

5. **Bucket 5**: Core audit
   - AA-02, AA-03, AA-05 (mandatory)
   - AA-01 (recommended)
   - AA-04 (risk-based)

**Total for QFCRA**: 23 controls (existing)

### For Entities Regulated by Both

**Activation Strategy**:

1. Activate UNION of QCB + QFCRA controls (all 26 base controls)
2. Add all 8 QCB-specific new controls
3. Apply STRICTEST parameters:
   - 10-year retention (not 6)
   - 12.5% CAR (not 10.5%)
   - Monthly reporting (not just quarterly)
   - Bilingual documentation
   - Full AAOIFI adoption
4. Dual reporting to both regulators

**Total for Both**: 34 controls (maximum set)
