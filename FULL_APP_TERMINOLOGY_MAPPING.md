## 9. Developer-Only Terms (Do Not Show to Users)

These terms should NEVER appear in user-facing UI:

- `useState`, `useEffect`, `useWorkflowStore` (React hooks)
- `async/await`, `Promise`, `callback` (JavaScript)
- `props`, `state`, `component` (React concepts)
- `API key`, `Bearer token`, `JWT` (Auth internals)
- `localhost`, `127.0.0.1`, `port 3000` (Dev environment)
- `npm`, `yarn`, `node_modules` (Package management)
- `Git`, `commit`, `branch`, `pull request` (Version control)
- `Docker`, `container`, `image` (Deployment)
